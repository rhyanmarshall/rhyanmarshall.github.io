03 / 05

Study Hive Website

Database and Site Development

Collaboratively designed and developed Study Hive, a productivity platform built for students to stay organized and focused throughout the semester. The site features a relational database where students can log and track assignments across all courses, storing key details like due dates, course names, required materials, and personal notes. To enhance the study experience, Study Hive integrates a Spotify Study Playlist API for shuffled study music and includes a Pomodoro Timer to help students manage focus intervals and reduce mental fatigue. Built using React, Supabase, and Tailwind CSS, the project demonstrates full-stack development skills and a strong focus on user experience.

UX/UI Prototype

05 / 05

Growing Up Online

Research & Analysis

Authored an independent research paper examining how social media trends are reshaping the values, behaviors, and self-identity of younger generations. The paper explored the relationship between trend exposure, behavioral adoption, and internalized identity — drawing on existing literature and a proposed survey-based case study spanning multiple age groups. Findings were designed to be visualized through bar graphs and scatter plots to measure correlations between key variables. The research highlights a growing concern about the long-term effects of unrestricted social media access on childhood development and identity formation.
